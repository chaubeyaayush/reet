About Our Company

Founded in 2021 by Er. Shakti Madhesiya, Er. Rahul Akhand, Er. Rahul Choudhary, and Er. Rohan Raghuvanshi, Reet Associates was established to meet the dire need for consultancy firms in India that adhere to high international standards and can address the nation's multi-disciplinary technical requirements in Electrical, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering.

We leverage India's vast pool of highly qualified engineers, architects, and technicians, providing them with opportunities to contribute to the development and services of our country in areas like public utilities, community services, industrial projects, and infrastructural development.

Our vision is to become a large and leading company, built on shared values and unmatched ambitions, leaving a lasting legacy for future generations of professionals.

Reet Associates is rapidly growing and is renowned for excellence in providing electrical solutions, continuously expanding its in-house expertise, technology, and scope of consultancy services across India.
